Company

STACK BRANDS is a multi-brand e-commerce holding company specializing in direct-to-consumer fashion. We acquire, build, and scale online retail brands across global markets, with a focus on operational efficiency and sustainable growth.

What We Do

We operate a portfolio of independent fashion brands, each serving distinct customer segments across North America, Europe, and the United Kingdom. Our centralized infrastructure handles supply chain management, performance marketing, customer support, and fulfillment, allowing each brand to scale independently while sharing operational resources.

Operations

Our operations are driven by proprietary automation systems and data-driven decision making. From order processing and supplier management to advertising optimization and financial reporting, we use technology to reduce manual overhead and improve accuracy at every stage of the value chain.
