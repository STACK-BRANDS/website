Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize your device and remember certain information about your visit, such as your authentication state and preferences.

2. How We Use Cookies

STACK BRANDS uses cookies exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: to maintain your login session on app.stack-brands.com and verify your identity across page loads. These cookies are strictly necessary for the platform to function.
  • Security: to protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF) and other security threats.
  • Preferences: to remember interface settings such as display mode and navigation state.

We do not use cookies for advertising, tracking across third-party websites, or behavioral profiling.

3. Types of Cookies We Use
  • Session cookies: temporary cookies that expire when you close your browser. Used for authentication session management.
  • Persistent cookies: cookies that remain on your device for a set period. Used for remembering authentication state between sessions, with a maximum retention of 30 days.
  • First-party cookies only: all cookies are set by stack-brands.com or app.stack-brands.com directly. We do not use third-party tracking cookies.
4. Third-Party Cookies

Our platform uses Google OAuth for authentication. Google may set cookies as part of the OAuth flow. These cookies are governed by Google's Cookie Policy. We do not control these cookies and do not receive the data they collect beyond what is necessary for authentication.

5. Managing Cookies

You can control and delete cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling authentication cookies will prevent you from logging in to the STACK BRANDS platform.

Most browsers allow you to:

  • View cookies stored on your device.
  • Delete all or specific cookies.
  • Block cookies from specific websites.
  • Block all cookies (note: this will break authentication).
6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.
